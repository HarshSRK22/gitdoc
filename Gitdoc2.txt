Pat Cummins (born May 8, 1993) is a premier Australian international cricketer and the current captain of the Australian Test and One Day International (ODI) teams. Known as one of the world's best fast bowlers, he led Australia to victory in the 2023 ODI World Cup and the 2021-23 World Test Championship.

Key Highlights & Career
Captaincy: Appointed Test captain in late 2021 and ODI captain in 2022, leading the side to major ICC titles.

Bowling Style: Right-arm fast bowler known for pace, accuracy, and taking over 500 international wickets.

Awards: Awarded the Allan Border Medal in 2019 as Australia's best player.

IPL Career: As of May 2026, he is the captain of the Sunrisers Hyderabad in the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Off-field: He is involved in environmental sustainability through his initiative "Cricket for Climate" and is a UNICEF ambassador.In May 2026, he is actively playing in the IPL and was rested for Australia's white-ball tour of Pakistan and Bangladesh.
